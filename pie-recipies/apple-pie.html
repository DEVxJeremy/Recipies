Apple Pie Recipe!

First we will start with making the pie crust from scrartch

Ingredients needed for pie crust:

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 2 tsp of salt
  • 3/4 cup of crisco (packed down)
  • 2 1/2 oz of water

    • Instruction

    Mix all Ingredients together really well

    Add flour to the countertop where you plan to roll out the pie crust

    Roll out pie crust and place into your pie dish

    After you have placed the pie crust in the pie dish, make sure you poke holes all over the crust with a fork to prevent bubbles from forming when it bakes

    Place the prepared pie crust in the oven to bake for 45 minutes at 350 degree F

    After the pie crust is finished cooking and golden brown, take it out of the oven
